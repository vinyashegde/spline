Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of Shorto. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

Shorto does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not track your browsing activities or store any data that could identify you. The URLs you shorten are processed locally in your browser and are not sent to any server unless you explicitly use an external API.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not use or share any information about you. Our extension operates entirely within your browser, ensuring that your data stays private and secure.

Third-Party Services

Shorto may use third-party APIs for URL shortening (e.g., t.ly). These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how they handle your information.

Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Although our extension does not collect any personal information, we take measures to ensure that all operations performed by Shorto are secure and do not expose your data to unauthorized parties.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
